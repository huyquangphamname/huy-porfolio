The Challenge: The "Out-of-Stock" Drop-off

High demand for specific SKUs led to frequent stock-outs (OOS). User behavior analysis showed that 65% of users abandoned their entire cart when a single key item was unavailable.

My Role: Feature Monitoring

  • Defined metrics for the "Replacement Recommendation" algorithm.
  • Built pipelines to track "Suggestion Acceptance Rate" vs. "Abandonment."
  • Analyzed Channel Referral data to see which marketing channels drove high-intent traffic to low-stock items.
